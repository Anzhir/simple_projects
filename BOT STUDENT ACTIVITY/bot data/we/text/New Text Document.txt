IEEE Woman In Engineering Helwan Affinity Group (WIE HAG) is promoting Women in society either in Technical or in Non-Technical Fields.
From the standpoint of promoting women in society, IEEE Woman In Engineering is a global network of IEEE members and volunteers dedicated to promoting women engineers technology for the benefit of humanity.
IEEE WIE HAG strives to two main categories:
1) WIE Juniors that works on empowering schoolgirls through Technical and Non-Technical sessions followed by workshops.
2) WIE Extreme that works on developing college girls through Non-technical sessions followed by workshops.
